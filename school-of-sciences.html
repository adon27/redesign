About the School

Shaping the Scientists
of Tomorrow

The School of Sciences at CHRIST University Lavasa has created a distinguished niche in higher education through innovative programmes in Computer Science and Statistics & Data Science. The school blends rigorous academics with hands-on research, making it an ideal destination for students with a research bent of mind.

Research and publication, even at the undergraduate level, are integral parts of these programmes. Funded research projects from agencies like UGC, DST, ISRO, SERB, NCBS and KSTA underscore the school's commitment to cutting-edge discovery.

A dedicated research block with the latest facilities boosts the morale of faculty and research scholars alike. Students are moulded holistically through various co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
