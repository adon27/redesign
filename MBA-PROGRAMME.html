Programme Overview

About the Programme

The MBA Programme at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Lavasa is a full-time, two-year programme offered over six trimesters, designed to prepare students for leadership roles in a dynamic and data-driven business environment. The programme delivers a rigorous, industry-relevant curriculum that integrates management fundamentals with analytical and strategic depth.

Residential Learning Community

Students and faculty live on campus, creating a close-knit academic community where learning continues beyond formal class hours through peer bonding, faculty connect, and a culture of collaboration.

International Exposure

A 1+1 dual degree pathway, international faculty visits, global academic collaborations, and a Global Immersion Program provide real international exposure to curricula, work culture, and diversity.

Year-long Part-time Internship

A distinctive year-long part-time internship during the second year allows students to pursue live projects, consulting assignments, and research initiatives within a flexible academic structure.

Theatre in Management (TiME)

TiME integrates applied drama, role-play, and reflective performance to develop communication skills, empathy, self-awareness, and human-centred leadership — unique to CHRIST Lavasa.

Why MBA at CHRIST Lavasa

Why Choose This Programme?

Experiential Pedagogy

Teaching and assessment are built around case studies, simulations, live projects, and continuous evaluation — ensuring practical and outcome-driven learning aligned with top global B-schools.

Research-Oriented Curriculum

Students engage in application-oriented research linked to real business challenges, strengthening analytical thinking and evidence-based decision-making across all specialisations.

Diverse Peer Cohort

A diverse student cohort from across disciplines and geographies enhances peer learning, collaborative thinking, and cross-functional problem-solving — a hallmark of the Lavasa experience.

Business Simulations

Simulated business environments and gamified learning build strategic thinking and competitive problem-solving skills that replicate real-world decision pressure.

Corporate Exposure

Field visits, corporate interactions, and live industry projects bridge the gap between classroom learning and industry practices, preparing graduates for immediate professional impact.

Ethical Leadership Focus

Beyond academics, the programme develops ethical leadership, managerial competence, and self-awareness — rooted in CHRIST University's vision of Excellence and Service.

Areas of Specialisation

Choose Your Track

From the third trimester onwards, students deepen their expertise by choosing from three high-demand specialisation domains. Each track is designed in collaboration with industry partners to reflect current business needs.

Finance

Financial analytics, investment banking, derivatives, valuation, risk management, and financial econometrics.

Marketing

Consumer behaviour, marketing metrics, digital strategy, brand management, and data-driven market research.

Business Analytics

Machine learning, business intelligence, data visualisation, predictive modelling, and Python-based analytics.
