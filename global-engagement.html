About

Global Engagement at CHRIST Lavasa

The Global Engagement Office (GEO) at CHRIST University Lavasa is the bridge between our students and the wider world. We facilitate semester exchanges, dual-degree programmes, short-term study abroad, summer schools, visiting faculty programmes and joint research collaborations with leading universities across Asia, Europe, North America and Australia.

Whether you want to spend a semester abroad, earn a dual degree, or bring in global perspectives through visiting faculty — GEO is your starting point.

Outbound Exchange
CHRIST Lavasa students study at partner universities abroad for a semester or year.
Inbound Exchange
International students join our campus for one or two semesters, enriching our community.
Dual Degree
Earn two degrees — one from CHRIST Lavasa and one from a partner university abroad.

Application Process

How to Apply for an Exchange

1
Step 1 · November–December
Check Eligibility

Minimum CGPA of 7.0, no active backlogs, and completion of at least 2 semesters at CHRIST Lavasa. Specific partner universities may have additional language or course requirements.

2
Step 2 · January
Attend GEO Orientation

Attend the mandatory Global Engagement Orientation session held each January. Programme details, deadlines, partner brochures and credit mapping guides are distributed here.

3
Step 3 · February
Submit Internal Application

Fill the GEO internal nomination form. Your Head of Department and Dean must sign off. Shortlisting is based on CGPA, statement of purpose, and faculty recommendation.

4
Step 4 · March
Partner University Nomination

GEO nominates shortlisted students to partner universities. Each partner conducts its own selection. You may be asked for a video interview, additional essays or language test scores.

5
Step 5 · April–May
Visa & Pre-Departure

GEO assists with visa documentation, travel insurance, accommodation guidance and pre-departure briefings. Financial aid and scholarship applications are processed during this stage.

6
Step 6 · Upon Return
Credit Transfer

Official transcripts from the host university are submitted to the CHRIST Lavasa Registrar. GEO facilitates credit transfer in line with the pre-approved Learning Agreement signed before departure.

Financial Support

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Going global shouldn't be a financial barrier. Several funding sources are available to CHRIST Lavasa students applying for exchange programmes.

CHRIST University
CHRIST Global Merit Scholarship

Partial tuition waiver for exchange students with CGPA ≥ 8.5. Covers up to 50% of host university tuition fees. Awarded by the Academic Council.

Up to ₹1,20,000
Government of India
ICCR & Inlaks Scholarship

Government scholarships for study abroad in select partner countries. GEO assists with documentation and endorsement letters required for these competitive awards.

Variable · Competitive
Partner Universities
Host Institution Waivers

Many partner universities offer tuition fee waivers to exchange students from CHRIST. Accommodation subsidies are also available at select partners in Germany, Japan and Australia.

Full / Partial Tuition Waiver
DAAD · Erasmus+ · JASSO
External Government Scholarships

International Students

Incoming Exchange Students

CHRIST University Lavasa warmly welcomes international exchange students from our partner institutions. Set against the stunning backdrop of the Sahyadri hills in Lavasa, our campus offers an unmatched environment for learning and personal growth.

As an incoming student you will have access to all academic facilities, hostel accommodation, the Lavasa library, sports facilities and our active student community.
