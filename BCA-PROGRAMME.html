Programme Overview

About the Programme

Our BCA programme is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the fast-paced world of computer science — covering both practical and theoretical aspects to prepare you for the challenges and opportunities ahead.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Solid CS foundation with flexibility to explore elective courses. Interdisciplinary studies in Mathematics, Statistics, and Financial Management broaden your horizons.

Expert Faculty & Facilities

Learn from dedicated faculty. State-of-the-art labs with 24×7 access, AI/ML Lab, IoT Lab, and Bloomberg Terminal — world-class hands-on resources.

Real-World Experience

In-house projects, research and consultancy work. Your sixth-semester capstone can lead to groundbreaking contributions or even patents.

Global Pathways

Full-time industry internships or credit transfers at prestigious foreign universities through our student exchange programmes.

Message from HOD

Dr Lija Jacob
Head of Department
Computer Science

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Pune Lavasa Campus. Our BCA programme is built on the philosophy of creating a dynamic and experiential learning environment that goes beyond textbooks.

We provide 24×7 campus access to our state-of-the-art labs, enabling students to work on real-world projects, industry collaborations, and cutting-edge research. Our faculty bring both academic excellence and industry experience to the classroom.

I warmly invite you to be part of our vibrant academic community — where we aim to shape the future with technology, developing not just skilled programmers, but responsible, creative, and globally aware professionals.

— Dr Lija Jacob, HOD, Department of Computer Science
